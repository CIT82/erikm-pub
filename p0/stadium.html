Stadium Overview
Gillette Stadium
Home of the New England Patriots

Gillette Stadium

Opened in 2002, located in Foxborough, Massachusetts, home to the Patriots and Revolution.

Capacity

Seating capacity of approximately 65,878 for football games.

Design

Designed by HNTB, featuring a distinctive open-air design with views of the surrounding area.

Events

Hosts concerts, college football, and other major events, alongside NFL games.

Accessibility

Fully accessible for all fans, with features like elevators and dedicated seating areas.

Parking

Offers ample parking space with shuttle services for major events.

Food and Beverage

Features a variety of food and beverage options, including local vendors.

Memorabilia

Home to various Patriots' memorabilia and exhibits celebrating the team's history.
