Stadium Overview
Gillette Stadium
Home of the New England Patriots

Gillette Stadium

Opened in 2002, located in Foxborough, Massachusetts, home to the Patriots and Revolution.

Capacity

Seating capacity of approximately 65,878 for football games.

Design

Designed by HNTB, featuring a distinctive open-air design with views of the surrounding area.

Events

Hosts concerts, college football, and other major events, alongside NFL games.

Accessibility

Fully accessible for all fans, with features like elevators and dedicated seating areas.

Transportation

Easy access via highways, public transportation, and ample parking for attendees.

Iconic Games

Witnessed unforgettable moments in Patriots history, including Super Bowl victories.

Fan Experience

Known for an unparalleled fan experience, with food, entertainment, and more.

Notable Patriots Players

Dont'a Hightower

Key player in the Patriots' defense, known for his leadership and versatility.

Stephon Gilmore

Shutdown cornerback, former Defensive Player of the Year, and a cornerstone of the defense.
