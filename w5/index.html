Why Dropdowns?

Button dropdowns come in various sizes, including default and split options. I chose dropdowns because they offer a streamlined way to organize and access multiple choices or actions from a single button. This method is particularly beneficial for websites with extensive content or features, such as large online stores, detailed news sites, and interactive portfolios. Dropdowns help reduce clutter by consolidating related links into a single menu, making it easier for users to find what they need quickly. For example, on a busy news site, dropdowns can categorize articles by topics like sports, politics, or entertainment. On an online store, they can filter products by brand, price range, or type. By grouping similar options together, dropdowns enhance user experience, making navigation more intuitive and less overwhelming. This approach helps maintain a clean and organized interface, which is crucial for user satisfaction and efficient browsing.

Sizing

Button dropdowns work with buttons of all sizes, including default and split dropdown buttons.
